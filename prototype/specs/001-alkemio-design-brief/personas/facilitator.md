# Persona: Facilitator

## Role Summary
Project manager / collaboration facilitator / process guide; keeps stakeholders informed and aligned strategically and operationally.

## Goals & Motivation
- Align stakeholders on project purpose
- Align stakeholders on process and expectations
- Set up the initiative effectively for productive collaboration
- (Founder) Empower others to drive the topic forward

## Jobs To Be Done
- Define clear roles and responsibilities per stakeholder
- Operationalize agreed way of working, priorities, and high-level strategy
- Keep stakeholders informed of important decisions
- Provide stakeholders access to relevant information and outputs
- Delegate and structure information
- (Founder) Enable community to connect; ensure continuity within topics

## Pain Points
- Time drain from answering repeated questions
- Difficulty aligning people due to scattered information across platforms
- Hard to see/reuse what worked in past projects because information is scattered
