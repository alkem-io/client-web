# Persona: Portfolio Owner

## Role Summary
Strategic owner of a portfolio of multi-stakeholder collaborations; enables cross-learning and reuse of best practices; not involved in daily work.

## Goals & Motivation
- Showcase organization’s work to find new partners and projects
- Increase organizational effectiveness to make more impact/progress
- Scale collaborations and replicate successful best practices

## Pain Points
- Hard to obtain real-time project status because data is scattered across platforms
- Hard to analyze overall performance for same reason
- Hard to facilitate knowledge sharing and capture lessons learned because information is scattered
- Hard to systematize/standardize ways of working across projects; each uses its own tools/approach

## Jobs To Be Done
- Oversee the portfolio to make informed strategic decisions
- Track project performance to intervene when needed
- Facilitate knowledge sharing to avoid duplication
- Capture lessons learned so new projects don’t start from scratch
- Show/share best practices for others to reuse
- Ensure the right people are in place with continuity
