# Persona: Active Stakeholder (Contributor)

## Role Summary
Actively involved stakeholder contributing insights and collaborating; ensures their organization is represented in project decisions.

## Goals & Motivation
- Make meaningful contributions that create visible progress
- Bring expertise and insights back to their organization
- Ensure their organization is represented in decisions

## Jobs To Be Done
- Provide insights and feedback on ongoing work
- Engage in discussions and brainstorming
- Connect with other stakeholders to expand network
- Bring organization’s perspective and goals into problem framing and solutions

## Pain Points
- Information is scattered, hard to see where to contribute
- Unclear what happened with their input
- Unclear agendas/goals of other participants

## Other
- Platform role: Member
- Membership: 3 Spaces, 3 Subspaces each
- Resources: none
